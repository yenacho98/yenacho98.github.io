Welcome

I am interested in understanding how individuals navigate stress, recovery, and attention in increasingly digital and boundaryless work environments.

About

My research sits at the intersection of occupational health psychology, cognition, and technology. I examine how employees recover from daily stressors and maintain focus in environments shaped by constant connectivity.

Using daily diary and experience sampling methods, I study within-person fluctuations in recovery, attention, and well-being across time.

Research Interests

Workplace Breaks & Recovery

How microbreaks support recovery and performance during the workday.

Social Media & Work

How digital platforms blur boundaries between work and nonwork.

Self-Regulation & Attention

How executive function shapes focus and recovery in digital environments.
